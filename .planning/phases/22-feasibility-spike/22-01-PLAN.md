Build the KEPT, re-runnable benchmark harness under `benchmarks/` (outside `src/`) that empirically measures GIL release for two ADBC operations through the real `create_pool(DuckDBConfig(...))` checkout path, plus a pure-function unit test for the harness arithmetic.
