`AsyncPool` (owning its dedicated `CapacityLimiter`), and the `create_async_pool` /
`managed_async_pool` / `close_async_pool` entry points â€” all reusing the **unchanged** sync

the `AsyncConnection`/`AsyncCursor` **contracts** (class skeletons with typed signatures and
`raise NotImplementedError` bodies, or minimal constructors) here so Plan 03 fills in
behavior against a fixed interface (interface-first ordering).
