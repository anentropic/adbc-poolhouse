## Decisions Made
- Used direct method implementation (not Pydantic model_dump with aliases) for Snowflake's to_adbc_kwargs(). The Snowflake translator has complex field-to-key mappings (user->username, private_key_path->adbc.snowflake.sql.client_option.jwt_private_key, port needs str() conversion) that don't map cleanly to Field aliases. Direct implementation is clearer and matches the existing translator 1:1.
- Added a try/except NotImplementedError fallback in translate_config() so unmigrated backends continue working through the registry translator during the consolidation transition. This avoids breaking all non-Snowflake dispatch tests.
